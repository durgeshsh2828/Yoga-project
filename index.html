Yoga Training

The word “Yoga” essentially means, “that which brings you to reality”. Literally, it means “union.” Union means it brings you to the ultimate reality, where individual manifestations of life are surface bubbles in the process of creation.

Dhanurasana, also known as Bow Pose, is a powerful backbend that opens the chest, stretches the abdomen, and strengthens the back muscles. Named after the Sanskrit word "Dhanu," meaning bow, this pose resembles an archer's bow when performed correctly.

Yoga Training

Shirshasana, also known as Headstand, is often referred to as the "king of asanas" due to its numerous physical and mental benefits. This inversion pose involves balancing the body on the head with the support of the forearms, promoting circulation and concentration.

Yoga Training

Chakrasana, also known as Wheel Pose, is a dynamic backbend that opens the chest, strengthens the spine, and energizes the body. Named after the Sanskrit word "Chakra," meaning wheel, this pose resembles the shape of a wheel when fully expressed.
